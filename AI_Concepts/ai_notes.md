# AI Concepts and Notes 🚀

# 1. Introduction to AI

AI is divided into major fields:

- ML → Machine Learning
- DL → Deep Learning
- CV → Computer Vision
- NLP → Natural Language Processing

## Definitions

- AI mimics or copies human intelligence.
- AI works based on data.
- “If no data, no AI.”
- Deep Learning is considered the brain of AI.
- Computer Vision gives “eyes” to computers.
- NLP helps machines understand human language.
- Machine language works in binary format (0 and 1).

# 2. Traditional AI vs Generative AI

## AI History

- Alan Turing is considered one of the inventors of AI.
- Neural Networks became popular around 2016.

## Rule-Based AI

Traditional AI uses:
- if-else conditions
- fixed rules
- pattern matching

Features:
- Performs one task
- Stores data
- Follows programmed logic

## Generative AI

Generative AI creates new content that does not already exist.

It can generate:
1. Images
2. Videos
3. Code
4. Audio

Examples:
- ChatGPT
- Gemini

# 3. Agentic AI

## Agentic AI

- Advanced form of AI
- Can make decisions independently
- Acts autonomously

## Difference

- Normal AI → Works only on assigned tasks
- Agentic AI → Makes decisions like humans

## Applications

- Healthcare
- Finance
- Customer Service
- Education

## Agent

An AI agent can work without human intervention.
